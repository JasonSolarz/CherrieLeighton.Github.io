Cherrie Leighton

A loving mother and grandmother

Cherrie Leighton, on the left, with her daugher Allyshia Leighton

    Here's a timeline of Cherrie Leighton's life:

  • 1959 - Born on September 20th in New London, CT at Lawrence Memorial Hospital. Daughter of Ruth Pomroy and Richard Pomroy
  • 1971 - Moved to Sedalia, Missouri, to live with her grandmother Ruth Bachmann
  • 1978 - On September 30th, Cherrie got married to Raymond Pepin.
  • 1979 - Started first job picking mushrooms at the Franklin Mushroom Farm in Franklin, Connecticut. She used the money to rent her first apartment on her own in Jewett City, CT. She also completed driving school, got her license and purchased her first car - A Ford Falcon. Unfortunately, that car was a lemon, so she financed her second car - A Plymouth Duster.
  • 1981 - Her grandmother, Ruth Bachmann, passed away in October.
  • 1982 - Married Michael Solarz in November in Montville, CT and they enjoyed their honeymoon in Freeport, Bahamas. She also started her second job sorting and cutting pictures at ABC Film Co. in Mystic, CT.
  • 1983 - Her first son was born, Jason Solarz, at Lawrence & Memorial Hospital in New Londont, CT.
  • 1986 - Graduated Norwich adult education and aquired her high school GED
  • 1988 - Married Randy Leighton on June, 30th in Norwich, CT, and started 3rd job as a Puncher for wired phone parts at Ortronics in Greenville, CT.
  • 1989 - Second child was born, Allyshia Leighton at Backus Hospital in Norwich, Connecticut.
  • 1992 - Third child was born, Edward Leighton at Backus Hospital In Norwich, Connecticut
  • 2000 - Started 4th job at Norwich Public Schools in the cafeteria while working the Norwich Ten Pin part time.
  • 2009 - Got to witness her first granddaughter, Avalynn, being born on February 10th at Backus Hospital by her daughter Allyshia.
  • 2011 - Her caring mother, Ruth Pomroy, passed away in July.
  • 2013 - Her stoic father, Richard Pomroy, passed away in February.
  • 2014 - Got to witness her first grandson, Leo Madore, being born on July 1st at Backus Hospital by her daughter Allyshia.
  • 2015 - Daughter Allyshia got married to Jon Madoare on November 15th.
  • 2016 - With strong work ethic and dedication, she became the Kitchen Manager at Veterans Memorial School of Norwich, Connecticut.
  • 2017 - Second grandson was born, Neil Madore, at Backus Hospital on April 17th
